Matteo Pagliero

pagliero [at] imada.sdu.dk

Since September 2025 I am a postdoc in the Operator Algebras group at the University of Southern Denmark (SDU), in Odense. I obtained my PhD in 2025 from KU Leuven, where my research was supported by a PhD-grant from the Research Foundation Flanders (FWO).

I am primarily interested in structure and classification of C*-algebras and their dynamical analogue. My current work focuses on the classification of amenable actions of locally compact groups on non-simple C*-algebras. Scroll down to see my publications and preprints.
