Welcome to my website



I am a PhD student in Mathematics at KU Leuven, under the supervision of Gábor Szabó. I am currently supported by an FWO (Research Foundation Flanders) PhD Fellowship Fundamental Research, grant number 1131623N. My main interests are in C*-algebras and C*-dynamical systems. At the moment, I am working on the classification of group actions on C*-algebras.

I obtained my BSc and MSc degree from the University of Turin, where I was also a fellow at SSST (School of Advanced Studies of Turin).
