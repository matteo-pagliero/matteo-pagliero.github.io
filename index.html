Matteo Pagliero

matteo.pagliero [at] kuleuven.be

I am a postdoc in the Operator Algebras group at SDU, in Odense. I obtained my PhD at KU Leuven, where my research was supported by a PhD-grant from the Research Foundation Flanders (FWO).

I am primarily interested in structure and classification of C*-algebras and their dynamical analogue. My current work focuses on the classification of amenable actions of locally compact groups on non-simple C*-algebras.
