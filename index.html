Matteo Pagliero

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at KU Leuven, under the supervision of Gábor Szabó. My research is supported by an FWO (Research Foundation Flanders) PhD Fellowship Fundamental Research (grant number 1131623N).

My main research interests are structure theory and classification of C*-algebras and C*-dynamical systems. My current work focuses on the classification of amenable actions of locally compact groups on non-simple C*-algebras.
